About Us

Welcome to CIA.

Get the most advanced IT skills and technologies with us.

Confucius IT Academy of India is one of the biggest and premier computer science academies in India. It was established in 2012 in Bangalore with branch campuses in Kaltaka and Jaipur which appeared in 2018 respectively.

Over the years, CIA has formed academic collaborations with numerous renowned universities in developed countries such as the USA, UK, Canada, Germany and Japan, covering a range of subjects in computer science field

The vision statement of CIA given below reflects its deep conviction for high quality inclusive education: to remain always inclusive and quality conscious, and with deep conviction that knowledge not only improves the quality of life, but leads to good character, to capitalize on our inherent advantages to generate skilled manpower for nation building through excellent teaching, attracting talent, fostering creativity, research, and innovation.

Our mission is:
- To provide free and conducive atmosphere for creative thinking and impart deep disciplinary knowledge with an interdisciplinary bandwidth to the learners in order to make them problem solvers, leaders and entrepreneurs
- To undertake research in frontier and emerging areas to advance knowledge and through innovation turn it into intellectual property and entrepreneurship to accelerate growth
- To attract, retain and engage talent in diverse disciplines and promote academic rigour and scholarship
